Energy efficiency

Unlike conventional extractor fans which wastefully extract expensively produced heat, Heat Recovery Ventilators recover exhaust heat. The world is becoming increasingly aware of the enormous cost of energy production, and this, plus the use of fossil fuels to generate power, is a significant environmental consideration. Extractor fans potentially waste over a million kilowatts of energy every year. This is comparable to the total output of two large power stations. Replacing conventional extractor fans with heat recovery systems could save at least half of that energy loss.
